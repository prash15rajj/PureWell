Mental Health Support

At PureWell, we are committed to providing comprehensive mental health resources. Below are additional supports and resources you can access:

Government Resources

  • Ontario – Find Mental Health Support: 1-866-797-0000
  • Government of Canada - Indigenous Mental Health: 1-855-242-3310
  • Hope for Wellness Help Line: 1-855-242-3310

Canadian Mental Health Association (CMHA)

  • CMHA – Provincial Mental Health Supports: 1-800-875-6213

Centre for Addiction and Mental Health (CAMH)

  • CAMH – Help and Resources: 1-800-463-2338

General Resources

  • BounceBack: 1-866-345-0224 - A guided self-help program for mild-to-moderate anxiety or depression.
  • ConnexOntario: 1-866-531-2600 - Free and confidential health services information.
  • 211 Ontario: 211 or 1-877-330-3213 - Information and referral services.
  • Talk Suicide: 1-833-456-4566 - Toll-free support for concerns about suicide.
  • Distress and Crisis Ontario: dcontario.org - Support and services.

Anti-Black Racism & Mental Health Resources

  • Across Boundaries: (416) 787-3007 - Holistic mental health care with an anti-racist framework.
  • Black Creek Community Health Centre: 416-249-8000 - Comprehensive care for health and well-being.
  • Black Health Alliance: blackhealthalliance.ca - Improving health in Black communities.
  • Rexdale Community Health Centre: 416-744-0066 - Primary health care and programs in Toronto.
  • TAIBU Community Health Centre: 416-644-3536 - Primary care and health promotion in GTA.
  • Women’s Health in Women’s Hands: whiwh.com - Health care for racialized women in Toronto.

Indigenous Resources

  • National Indian Residential School Crisis Line: 1-866-925-4419 - Support for former residential school students.
  • Hope for Wellness Help Line: 1-855-242-3310 - Immediate mental health counselling for Indigenous peoples.
  • Talk4healing (for Indigenous women): Toll-free and text: 1-855-554-4325 - Help and support for Indigenous women in multiple languages.
  • Lumino Health: luminohealth.sunlife.ca - Information and tools for mental health.

For additional mental health services in your community, visit: ontario.ca/page/mental-health-services
